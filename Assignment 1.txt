In this assignment, students will create an interactive web app, with animations using only CSS3 and HTML5 to help kindergarten students
learn the alphabets. The app should show the users alphabets and the words starting with them. Finally add a simple quiz where the kids
 can choose words corresponding  to the alphabets from multiple options.

The app will be a pure css3 2d solution with not more than 24 images, no JavaScript  and no flash.
Use many animations and simple interactions to make the learning experience fun for the kids.

For maximum marks use urdu alphabets.

Students will upload their solution and description document both on LMS and GitHub.
